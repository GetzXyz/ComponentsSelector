Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

FORGE AI PC Builder ("FORGE", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. FORGE is a client-side tool. Your budget and build preferences are processed locally in your browser and sent directly to Google's Gemini AI API to generate PC component recommendations. We do not maintain user accounts and do not store your inputs or build results on any server we operate.

2. Data Sent to Third Parties

When you generate a PC build, your specified budget and use-case preference are sent to Google Gemini AI via the Google Generative Language API. This transmission is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect limited technical data about font requests per their privacy policy.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

FORGE uses localStorage only to store your cookie consent preference (forge_cookie_consent). No personal data is stored. See our Cookie Policy for details.

4. Children's Privacy

FORGE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last Updated" date will reflect any changes. Continued use of FORGE after changes constitutes acceptance.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: pcbuilder@privacy.forgeai.pk
