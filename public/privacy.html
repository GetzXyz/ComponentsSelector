Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

FORGE AI PC Builder ("FORGE", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

FORGE is a client-side application. Your budget and build preferences are processed locally in your browser. We send only your specified parameters to Google's Gemini/Groq AI API and do not maintain server-side user accounts.

2. Data Sent to Third Parties

When you generate a build, your budget and use-case are sent to Groq AI via their API, governed by Groq's Privacy Policy. We also use Google Fonts.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We store only your consent preference in localStorage. No personal data is saved.

4. Your Rights

You can clear all stored data by clearing your browser's local storage.

5. Contact

Questions? Email: privacy@forge-ai.pk
