Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. FORGE uses localStorage (browser storage) rather than traditional cookies for any preference data.

Third-Party Storage

Google Fonts: Loading fonts from Google's CDN may result in Google setting cookies or logging your IP address per Google's cookie policy.

What We Do NOT Use

  • Analytics or tracking cookies (Google Analytics, Hotjar, etc.)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any cross-site tracking

Managing Storage

You can clear localStorage at any time through your browser's developer tools or settings. Clearing it will reset your cookie consent preference. This will not affect FORGE's core functionality.
