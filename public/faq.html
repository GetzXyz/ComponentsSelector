FAQ

What is FORGE AI PC Builder?+
FORGE is an AI-powered PC build recommendation tool optimized for the Pakistani market. Input your budget and use case, and FORGE uses Groq AI with live market research to recommend components at current Pakistani retail prices.
Are the prices accurate?+
FORGE fetches current market data from Pakistani retailers. However, prices fluctuate daily. Always verify directly with sellers before purchasing. FORGE is a research starting point.
What currencies are supported?+
FORGE supports PKR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SAR, CAD, and AUD. Select your currency and all prices convert automatically. PKR gives the most accurate Pakistani prices.
What is the minimum budget?+
Minimum is Rs 30,000 PKR. For proper gaming, we recommend at least Rs 80,000. Creative work requires Rs 150,000+.
Can I trust the recommendations?+
FORGE recommendations are AI-generated and compatibility-aware. However, always verify compatibility independently using tools like PCPartPicker before purchasing. FORGE is informational.
Is my data private?+
FORGE does not store your data. Your budget is sent only to Groq AI to generate recommendations. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Why did I get an error?+
Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, the Groq API may be temporarily unavailable. FORGE will show cached fallback recommendations which are still high-quality.
